To Commence With….

Technology is, in this modern world, the most powerful tool to build the future. The inextinguishable flame of innovation burns within every individual obsessed with technology. The Engineering college campus buzzes with new ideas harnessed by the torchbearers of tomorrow. The least that can be done for them is to bring those innovative minds together and provide a friendly environment for them to carry on their work of creating their own reality. AUTOBIT, The Official Robotics Club of Bengal Institute of Technology acts as one such Neverland for the innovative Peter Pans out there. Founded and maintained by the department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, AUTOBIT has been successful in achieving great success in supporting innovative technical ventures of students and securing prestigious positions at both national and international levels.
Excelling in the field of Robotics (Manual and Autonomous), this club strives to prioritize the development of innovative projects with a vision to shape a brighter and advanced future ahead. Knowledge from books is necessary, but never totally fulfilling and hence, AUTOBIT arranges for hands-on projects and seminars frequently joining hands with leading personalities and institutions in the technical field. Holding second position in the State after IIT Kharagpur in terms of achievements, this club is advancing towards uniting efficient hardware with strong software with a vision of integrated development of real-time projects. Lastly, let us all strive to an advanced future together with the motto of Sa Vidya Ya Vimukhtaye (Learning for Liberation).

-TEAM AUTOBIT

What's in the Box ?

Manual Robotics– You’re the seer, you hold a gear!

In this section we shall proceed towards building remotely controlled bots that operate at your own commands. Wired or wireless, these bots do whatever you ask them to do.

Autonomous Robotics– It’s no wonder, let the device ponder!

We believe an ideal bot is one that does its thinking on its own. As the name suggests, this section introduces you to the intriguing world of self-thinking bots. A typical collaboration of hardware and coding, these smart ones carry out own decision-making and do their assigned tasks without any human intervention.

Innovative Projects– Challenging Limitations for Fun

“I shouldn’t be alive unless it was for a reason.” – Tony Stark.
For us, engineers, the reason is same – we live to innovate, we love to innovate. Recognizing and acknowledging this indomitable spirit of engineers AUTOBIT provides facilities for dreamers to bring their dreams to life with innovative technology. A number of projects developed under this club by its members have gained national and international acclamations in the past two years.

IoT– Living the Internet

Internet of Things or IoT is a trending topic today that is responsible for connecting every entity to the internet, literally. Imagine having a world where all non-living things interact among each other just the way we humans do. Seems like a Utopian dream right? IoT is the science behind that magic. This club takes pride in being supporter of such endeavors of the students to build devices based on this technology.
